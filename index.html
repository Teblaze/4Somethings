Videogame AI

The Video gaming industry has come a long way since it was first implementing Artificial intelligence, in the past 50 years, only recently have we begun to scratch the surface on what we can accomplish with AI. It would be hard to imagine video games without AI as they have become such a large part of the industry, we barely even notice it half the time.
